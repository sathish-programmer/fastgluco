Request Account Deletion

Warning: Deleting your account is permanent. All your food logs, CGM reports, and health analysis data will be permanently erased from our servers.

You can delete your Mito Reboot account and all associated data at any time. There are two ways to do this:

Method 1: Inside the App (Fastest)

  1. Open the Mito Reboot app on your device.
  2. Go to the Profile tab in the bottom navigation.
  3. Scroll down to the Legal section and tap Data Deletion.
  4. Follow the prompts to permanently delete your account.

Method 2: Email Request

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request data deletion via email:

  1. Send an email to support@mitoreboot.in
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the phone number you used to register your account.

Note: For security reasons, we may require additional verification to prove ownership of the phone number before processing email requests.

Questions? Contact our support team at support@mitoreboot.in
